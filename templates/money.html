How Do You Make Money with Youtube?

Youtube is able to partner itself with any channel that fits its criteria (usually it's a specific number of views and subscribers to even be considered).

Once you're accepted into the program, Youtube will begin displaying ads on your videos. Depending on how much you make through these ads, Youtube will keep 45% and pay you the remaining 55%.


What determines how much money you make?

  • Country
    • Youtube puts different country's traffic into different tiers.
    • Traffic from countries like the US will pay more than countries like Brazil
  • Demographics
    • Traffic from minors or low income individuals will generate less money than industry professionals.
  • Video Niche
    • Ads are shown based on whether they relate to your video's content. As a result, an ad that's shown with a topic that's closer to the video's content will do better.
  • Types of Ads
    • Ads come in different shapes and sizes. Skippable ads will not generate revenue if they're skipped. Banner ads pay based on views, and pre-roll ads pay based on clicks.
  • Audience Attention
    • You make more money based on the number of people who finish your videos. This signals to advertisers that you have an active audience that's interested in your content.
  • Subscriber Count
    • The more subscribers you have, the more ad revenue you can make.

The big take away here is that there are multiple ways to make money on youtube's ads. But you don't need a large subscriber count or view count if you know how to monetize to your specific niche.

A majority of our top earners also have alternate revenue streams such as Twitch streams, Merchandise sales, and sponsorships to help them make more money than the rest of their competition.
